## Clean Properties, Happy Residents

Property managers need a cleaning partner that keeps common areas pristine, supports fast turnover, and delivers the reporting that boards and owners expect. We provide tailored cleaning for residential, condo, and mixed-use properties so you can focus on operations and retention.

### Why Property Managers Choose NFG

From lobbies and hallways to amenities and make-ready units, we deliver consistent quality that residents and boards notice. Our teams are trained for multi-family and condo environments, and we integrate with your staff so cleaning is seamless and accountable.

We know that cleanliness directly affects satisfaction, retention, and leasing. Our service is designed to meet property-level standards and provide the documentation you need for owners and boards.
