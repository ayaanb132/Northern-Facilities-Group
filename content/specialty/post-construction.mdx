# Professional Post-Construction Cleaning for Ontario Projects

Construction projects create mess. Dust, debris, adhesive residue, stained surfaces, hazed windows.

Your new facility should be move-in ready immediately. Instead, it needs a complete cleanup before anyone can occupy it. That's post-construction cleaning.

At Northern Facilities Group, we specialize in transforming construction sites into move-in-ready facilities. We handle the cleanup so you can focus on operations. Fast, thorough, professional.

**Construction ends. We make it move-in ready.**

## The Post-Construction Challenge

Construction creates three specific problems.

**Problem 1: Bulk Debris**
Drywall dust. Packaging material. Concrete residue. Scraps. Leftover materials. The site is literally buried in debris.
This needs to be removed before detailed cleaning can happen. It's labor-intensive and time-consuming.

**Problem 2: Dust Everywhere**
Construction dust is relentless. It settles on every surface—ceilings, walls, fixtures, equipment. Normal vacuuming doesn't reach it. Dust stays suspended and settles during operations, creating a perpetual mess.
You need specialized dust removal to clear air and surfaces completely.

**Problem 3: Contamination and Residue**
Paint splatters. Adhesive residue. Concrete residue on floors. Protective film on glass. Stains on surfaces. Construction leaves contamination that regular cleaning can't handle.
Specialized removal is needed to restore surfaces to clean condition.

## Why Post-Construction Cleaning is Critical

Post-construction cleaning isn't optional. It's essential.

**Move-In Readiness**
You can't move occupants or equipment into a dusty, debris-filled facility. Post-construction cleaning makes the space actually usable.

**Safety Compliance**
Construction leaves safety hazards. Scattered debris, slippery surfaces, hazardous materials. Proper cleanup removes hazards and prepares for safe operations.

**Equipment Protection**
If you're installing expensive equipment, it needs a clean environment. Dust damages electronics and machinery. Post-construction cleanup protects your investment.

**Health and Sanitation**
Construction dust is a health hazard. Respiratory risk, allergies, contamination. Complete cleanup ensures a safe, healthy environment.

**Professional Image**
New facilities should look pristine. Dust, debris, and residue damage the impression you make on clients, employees, and inspectors.

**Punch List Completion**
Contractors have punch lists—final items to complete before handoff. They don't handle post-construction cleaning. You need a professional cleaning company to finish what construction left incomplete.

## Our Post-Construction Cleaning Process

**Step 1: Initial Rough Clean (Bulk Debris Removal)**
We start by removing the bulk. Large debris, scraps, packaging, waste.

- All major debris is collected and removed
- Large items are disposed of properly
- Construction waste is organized and hauled away
- Floors are cleared of major debris
- Work areas are roughed in for detailed cleaning
  Timeline: Depends on project size. Small projects: 1 day. Large projects: 2-4 days. This step is labor-intensive but necessary before detail work.

**Step 2: Detailed Dust and Particle Removal**
Now we target construction dust that's settled everywhere. We clean high surfaces first (ceilings to floors) including light fixtures, air vents, walls, and equipment. We use HEPA vacuums, professional dust tools, air movers, and damp wiping. As dust is removed, air quality improves and the HVAC runs continuously to clear remaining particles.

**Step 3: Surface Cleaning and Residue Removal**
Now we handle contamination. Paint drips and splatter are carefully removed, adhesive and caulk residue is detached from floors, and tape stickers are scraped off. Concrete dust is scrubbed away. Oil, grease, and stains are treated, and protective coverings are peeled identically from all fixtures.

**Step 4: Window and Glass Cleaning (Interior)**
Windows are a construction mess. We restore them.
We clean interior glass (all windows and glass doors), frames and sills, target dirty window tracks, unpeel protective films, and fully remedy hazed glass.

**Step 5: Final Detail Cleaning and Inspection**
Before we're done, every detail is addressed. Detail work includes baseboards, custom trim, door frames, switch plates, outlets, hardware polishing, corner edges, and missed spots from earlier. Every surface is systematically inspected with a final quality verification pass.

**Step 6: Punch List Support**
We work with you to ensure punch list items are addressed. We identify any remaining construction issues, document them with photos, coordinate with contractors if needed, and guarantee a unified final walkthrough and approval.
