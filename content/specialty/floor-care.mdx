# Professional Floor Care and Restoration for Ontario Commercial Facilities

Your floors tell the story of your operation.

Worn, dull, or stained floors signal neglect. Professional, maintained floors signal excellence. But maintaining floors isn't simple—different floor types need different approaches, and most facilities don't have the expertise or equipment to do it right.

At Northern Facilities Group, we handle all floor types: VCT, hardwood, tile, concrete, carpet. We maintain them, restore them, and transform them from worn to professional.

**Your floors matter. We treat them that way.**

## Why Floor Care Actually Matters

Floors aren't just surfaces. They're part of your operational infrastructure.

**Safety**
Worn, slippery, or damaged floors are liability risks. Workers slip. Damage happens. Professional floor maintenance prevents accidents and reduces insurance risk.

**Operations**
A clean, well-maintained floor improves worker morale and productivity. Employees appreciate professional spaces. Clients notice. Operations run better in professional environments.

**Longevity**
Floors are expensive to replace. Proper maintenance extends their lifespan by years. Stripping and waxing VCT extends its life. Sealing concrete protects it. Extraction maintains carpet. Maintenance beats replacement.

**Appearance**
Your floors reflect your facility's standards. Worn floors look unprofessional. Maintained floors look operated. When clients or inspectors visit, your floors make an impression.

**Compliance**
Some facilities require documented floor maintenance for safety or sanitation compliance. We document everything. Audits pass.

## When You Need Floor Care Services

Different situations call for different floor care approaches.

- **Worn or Dull Hard Floors:** VCT, tile, or hardwood floors lose their shine over time. Waxing wears away. Finishes dull. A professional restoration restores them. Instead of replacing expensive flooring, restoration brings it back. Cost-effective. Immediate results.
- **Stained or High-Traffic Carpets:** Carpet accumulates stains, dirt, and wear. Daily vacuuming doesn't reach embedded dirt. Professional extraction removes deep dirt and restores carpet appearance. For high-traffic areas, extraction extends carpet life by years. For moving, extraction prepares carpet for inspection.
- **Annual Floor Maintenance Program:** Many facilities schedule quarterly or annual floor maintenance. Keeps floors looking professional. Prevents deterioration. Extends floor lifespan.
- **After Construction or Renovation:** Construction leaves floors filthy. Dust, debris, adhesive residue. Professional floor care cleans and restores floors to move-in condition.
- **Seasonal Refresh:** Spring/fall deep floor care resets floors after winter wear or summer use. Keeps floors looking professional year-round.
- **Before Inspections or Major Events:** Inspectors look at floors. Major events (client visits, grand openings) need pristine floors. Pre-event floor care ensures perfection.
- **Concrete Facility Floors:** Manufacturing, warehouses, and industrial facilities often have concrete. Unsealed concrete stains, deteriorates, becomes slippery. Sealing and polishing protects it and makes it safer.

## Floor Types We Handle

Different floors need different care. We handle them all.

**VCT (Vinyl Composition Tile)**
Most common commercial flooring. Durable but requires maintenance.

- **What we do:** Regular waxing, deep stripping and re-waxing, sealing, stain removal.
- **Why it matters:** VCT lasts 10-15 years with proper maintenance. Without it, replacement costs $3-5 per square foot. Stripping and waxing costs $0.50-1.50 per square foot. Maintenance beats replacement.

**Hardwood Floors**
More common in offices and higher-end facilities.

- **What we do:** Professional cleaning, refinishing, polyurethane application, spot restoration.
- **Why it matters:** Hardwood is expensive to replace. Professional cleaning prevents warping or damage.

**Tile and Grout**
Bathrooms, kitchens, and some commercial spaces.

- **What we do:** Tile cleaning, grout restoration, grout sealing, specialized stain removal.
- **Why it matters:** Tile is easy to clean. Grout is not. Professional grout restoration extends tile life and improves appearance dramatically.

**Concrete Floors**
Warehouses, manufacturing, garages, basements.

- **What we do:** Concrete polishing, sealing, degreasing, epoxy coating.
- **Why it matters:** Unsealed concrete stains easily, becomes slippery (safety hazard), and deteriorates. Sealed concrete is safer, lasts longer, looks professional.

**Carpet**
Offices, common areas, and some commercial spaces.

- **What we do:** Professional extraction, stain treatment, deodorization, fabric protection.
- **Why it matters:** Carpet hides embedded dirt. Vacuuming only removes surface dirt. Professional extraction removes 80% more dirt.

## Our Floor Care Process

**Step 1: Floor Assessment and Surface Identification**
We start by understanding your floors—type, condition, and specific needs. We evaluate the floor type, current condition, usage pattern, and your specific goals to create a customized care plan.

**Step 2: Furniture and Obstacle Clearing**
Before we start, we clear the floor. All moveable furniture is carefully moved, equipment is safely relocated, and work zones are established. You don't manage this. We do.

**Step 3: Surface Preparation and Stripping**
Different floors need different prep. For VCT, old wax is stripped. For hardwood, the floor is inspected and cleaned. For concrete, heavy contamination is degreased. For carpet, stains are pre-treated.

**Step 4: Treatment Application**
Now the actual floor care happens. Fresh wax is applied to VCT. Polyurethane to hardwood. Sealers applied to tile and concrete. For carpet, professional extraction removes deep dirt.

**Step 5: Buffing, Burnishing, and Finishing**
For hard floors, this step restores the shine. Floor buffers remove scuffs, and burnishing polishes the surface to a high shine. Protectors are allowed to cure.

**Step 6: Cure Time and Protection**
Different treatments need different cure times. VCT wax takes 24-48 hours before heavy foot traffic. Carpet protector takes 24 hours. We provide clear instructions.

**Step 7: Furniture Replacement and Final Inspection**
Once cured, everything goes back. Furniture is carefully replaced, the floor is inspected one final time, and we provide a client walkthrough for approval.
