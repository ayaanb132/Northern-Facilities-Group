Deliverables

Scope of work

  • Reception & waiting: counters, glass touch points, seating wipe-down
  • Boardrooms & break areas: tables, appliances (externals), handles, switches
  • Offices & open areas: desks (exposed), partitions, fixtures
  • Washrooms: full service, mirrors, consumables restock
  • Floors: HEPA vacuum + damp mop; edges & baseboards on rotation
  • Waste: removal, recycling; secure bins per policy

Add-ons on request: interior glass, fridge/microwave internals, dish runs, supply management (paper/soap).

Compliance

Protocols & compliance

  • Color-coded tools by zone to prevent cross-contamination
  • Health Canada DIN-registered disinfectants with observed dwell times
  • Signed SDS on site; staff trained on safe handling & PPE
  • Security: key control logs, alarm codes, access check-in/out
  • WSIB coverage & liability insurance (certificates available)

We align with Public Health Ontario environmental cleaning practices and high-touch sequencing best practices.

Scheduling

Scheduling & frequency

  • Nightly, multi-week, or daytime porter
  • Rotation detailing (glass, vents, baseboards)
  • Add-ons: electrostatic disinfection, steam, machine scrub
  • Emergency call-outs with defined response windows
