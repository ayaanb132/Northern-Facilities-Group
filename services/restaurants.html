Deliverables

Scope of work

  • FOH: tables/booths, bar tops, ledges, glass & mirrors
  • Entrance/mats: spot-clean and detail edges
  • Floors: sweep/HEPA vac + damp or machine scrub (as needed)
  • Washrooms: full service, consumables restock
  • BOH/staff: floors, handles/switches, break area, office
  • Rotation detailing: baseboards, vents, stainless polish

Exclusions on request: exhaust/hood/duct cleaning and grease trap servicing (we can coordinate).

Compliance

Protocols & safety

  • Food-service aware: avoid chemical overspray; proper rinses
  • DIN-registered disinfectants; dwell times observed
  • SDS on site; PPE; slip-hazard controls and signage
  • Security: key control, access logs, alarm codes
  • WSIB coverage & liability insurance (certificates available)

Grease-prone floors can be machine scrubbed; re-open guidance provided.

Scheduling

Scheduling & frequency

  • Nightly or multi-week after-hours cleans
  • Day porter for peak and spill control
  • Emergency call-outs with defined response windows
  • Rotation detailing for glass/baseboards
