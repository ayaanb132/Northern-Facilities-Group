Elevations we support

Low‑ to mid‑rise exteriors, retail façades, showrooms, atriums (interior/upper glass), and canopy glazing. Rope access on request where permitted.

Deliverables

Scope of work

  • Site plan & hazard review; barricades and spotters as needed
  • Pure‑water brush scrub & rinse; squeegee method where required
  • Frame, sill, and accessible ledge wipe
  • Adhesive/label residue and cobweb/wasp‑nest removal (accessible)
  • Final inspection at ground and representative upper panes
  • Post‑clean photos and punch‑list close‑out

Exclusions: scratch removal/glass restoration, failed IGU seal haze, hard‑water etching, and inaccessible glazing without approved access. Permit/traffic control fees passed at cost.

Compliance

Protocols & safety

  • Lift‑ticketed operators; fall‑protection trained
  • Daily equipment checks; tie‑off & outrigger use per manual
  • Weather stops: wind, lightning, and visibility thresholds
  • WSIB coverage & liability insurance (certificates)
  • Public protection: cones, barricades, spotters, signage

We coordinate with building management for off‑hours windows and traffic plans to minimize disruption.

  • Emergency call‑outs after storms or façade work
