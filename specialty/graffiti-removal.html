Surfaces we support

Brick, concrete, block, stucco, metal doors/frames, painted substrates, glass, signage (non‑vinyl), and select stone. Test patches performed first.

Deliverables

Scope of work

  • Assess paint/ink type and substrate porosity
  • Apply solvent gel or biodegradable remover as appropriate
  • Agitate and dwell; low‑pressure/steam or rinse to lift
  • Detail work on grout/mortar lines and textures
  • Neutralize residue; capture and dispose of runoff
  • Optional protective coating (sacrificial or permanent)

Exclusions: structural repairs, deep stone restoration, historic façade conservation beyond light cleaning. Colour matching of painted walls available as an add‑on where ghosting remains.

Compliance

Protocols & safety

  • WHMIS‑aligned; SDS on site; PPE; ventilation & ignition control
  • Wet‑vac/berm controls to prevent storm drain entry
  • Test patching to avoid etching or colour lift
  • WSIB coverage & liability insurance (certificates)
  • Public protection: cones, barricades, spotters, signage

We coordinate with building management for off‑hours windows and traffic plans to minimize disruption.
