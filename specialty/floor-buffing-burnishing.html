Floors we support

VCT/LVT, sheet vinyl, acrylic‑finished floors, sealed terrazzo (select), corridors, lobbies, retail, and classrooms.

Deliverables

Scope of work

  • Dust‑mop & HEPA pre‑vac; edge & corner detail
  • Spray buff with appropriate pad; micro‑repair & blend
  • High‑speed burnish to target gloss
  • Spot top‑coat repair (where feasible)
  • Final dust‑control & damp‑mop; signage removal
  • Optional anti‑slip/top‑coat enhancement

Exclusions: full strip & wax (see dedicated service), stone polishing, uneven floor repairs. Not recommended on rubber floors or unfinished concrete. We test pads/chemistry in a small area first.

Compliance

Protocols & safety

  • WHMIS‑aligned; SDS on site; PPE; cord/cable management
  • Wet‑floor signage; egress kept clear; noise windows honoured
  • Dust‑control vacs on burnishers to minimize particulates
  • WSIB coverage & liability insurance (certificates)
  • Slip‑coefficient awareness; finish compatibility checks

We coordinate with facilities to avoid peak traffic and manage elevator/lift access for equipment.
