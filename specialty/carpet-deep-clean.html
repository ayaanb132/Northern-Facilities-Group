Carpets we support

Commercial broadloom & carpet tile in offices, schools, clinics, retail, and common areas. Wool blends on request (test first).

Deliverables

Scope of work

  • Pre-vacuum (HEPA) and spot/stain identification
  • Pre-spray & agitation (CRB or brush) in traffic lanes
  • Method: HWE extraction or low-moisture encapsulation
  • Edge/detail passes and wick-back mitigation
  • Post-grooming for pile set; air movers as needed
  • Final walk-through and dry-time guidance

Exclusions: flood restoration, pad replacement, dye/bleach spot color repair. Delicate fibers and wool processed only with prior test and approval.

Compliance

Protocols & safety

  • IICRC S100 informed; SDS on site; PPE
  • Low-residue detergents to reduce re-soiling
  • Slip-hazard cones & cable management
  • WSIB coverage & liability insurance (certificates)
  • Noise/timing controls for occupied spaces

We select chemistry to match fiber type and backing to avoid delamination or dye transfer.

Scheduling

Scheduling & frequency

  • Quarterly or semi-annual for traffic lanes
  • Night work to meet dry-by-morning targets
  • Emergency stain call-outs
  • Rotation plan for multi-floor portfolios
