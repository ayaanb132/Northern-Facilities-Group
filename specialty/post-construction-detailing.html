Fit

Spaces we support

Tenant build‑outs, retail fit‑outs, clinic/office renovations, common‑area refreshes, and light industrial interiors.

Deliverables

Scope of work

  • Rough clean: debris pull, HEPA vacuum, major dust knock‑down
  • Adhesive/film/sticker removal; paint speck/silicone edge tidy
  • Interior glass and frame detail; hardware and fixtures
  • Floor protection pull; sweep/vac and damp/machine pass
  • Baseboards, millwork, ledges, vents—detail and wipe‑down
  • Final clean & punch‑list with deficiency notes/photos

Exclusions: construction waste hauling beyond site bins, damaged substrate repair, exterior façade cleaning. We can coordinate partners for glazing scratch removal or specialty coatings.

Compliance

Protocols & safety

  • Silica‑aware controls: wet methods, HEPA tools, containment
  • SDS on site; PPE; ladder/lift training; lock‑out/tag‑out as needed
  • Site orientation & permits respected; egress kept clear
  • WSIB coverage & $5M liability insurance (certificates)
  • Photo documentation of protected surfaces before work

We coordinate with trades to avoid re‑dusting; negative air and zone isolation available where required.

Scheduling

Scheduling & frequency

  • Phase‑aligned to your site schedule (rough/final/turnover)
  • After‑hours and weekend blocks to hit turnover dates
  • Emergency call‑outs with defined response windows
  • Rotation detailing for staged openings
